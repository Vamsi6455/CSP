Abstract

Cybersecurity has become an essential aspect of modern life as the digital landscape expands rapidly. Cyber-attacks pose a serious threat to individuals, organizations, and governments, leading to financial losses, identity theft, and breaches of sensitive information. Awareness of cyber threats enables individuals and organizations to implement preventive measures that reduce the risks associated with cybercrime. This paper explores the importance of cybersecurity awareness, common types of cyber-attacks, the role of education and training in mitigating risks, and the best practices that individuals and businesses can adopt to enhance their cybersecurity posture. By promoting awareness and proactive measures, society can build a more secure digital environment and protect critical information from malicious cyber activities.
